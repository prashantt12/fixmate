About FixMate

We are on a mission to organize the highly unorganized home services market by providing reliable, affordable, and high-quality services to every household.

Our Mission

Finding a trusted plumber, electrician, or cleaner is traditionally a hassle. You ask around, negotiate rates, and often end up with subpar work. FixMate was built to solve exactly this.

Our mission is to connect skilled, verified professionals with homeowners and renters who need immediate, high-quality assistance. We ensure that our pricing is transparent, our response is fast, and our work is guaranteed.

Quality Commitment

  • Vetted Professionals: Everyone on our platform goes through identity checks and skill validation.
  • Price Standardization: Standardized visit charges and predefined service costs. No sudden overcharging.
  • Service Guarantee: We stand by our work. If something goes wrong immediately after repair, we fix it at no extra cost.
